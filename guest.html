About Us

Seven Academy is a Silicon Valley blended First-Class IT Training Institute that trains young Cameroonians on highly-paid in-demand IT skills to enable them access high quality jobs worldwide. Located in Cameroon’s largest city Douala, Seven Academy is fast becoming a national leader in professional Web/Mobile Apps development, Digital Marketing, CyberSecurity, Cloud Computing, Data science training and others.
Our Curriculum
Is designed based on standard training rules from industry leaders notably Google, Amazon, MicroSoft , and institutions like Stanford and MIT considering local demand.

Our learning environment
Our training center is top notch. First, it is located in the heart of the economic capital Douala, Bali out of the Busy business district Akwa. Our training takes place in two top notch laboratories with capacity of 30 persons per lab, equipped with 62, 23 inch iMac computers each with decent computing power accompanied by training projectors and other gadgets. Our staff We have highly experienced staff with excellent professional and soft skills.

Seven Academy’s vision
Become a Continental Institution democratising access to quality technical training for Africans in a top learning environment mentored and taught by the best professionals in the industry.

Our Mission
We are set on a mission to provide highly paid in-demand IT skills to cameroonians and Africans that will enable them land high quality jobs worldwid. We want to be able to democratize quality technical training making it accessible to the least privileged so that they too can contribute value to society.
